My Fascination with ML

Ever since I was blown away by learning the existence of ML and its variants. I can clearly remember the day when I saw a video of Marl/io from sethbling, I was 10 at the time and this blew my mind. I vividly remember recreating the neural networks and genetic algorithm using his code in lua and getting it to connect to the emulator. Im pretty sure thats when I really decided to someday create a cool neural network of my own. And I did, several. I used ml is numerous in various projects of mine, whether it be for analyzing the sky to automatically find star arrangements, or even recently, using OpenAI and MuJuCo for doing simulations with drones to see how integration of ai with can help with pathfinding algorithms.

Many of my projects are available at my Github GitHub.
